About Me

I am a scientist and research software engineer in the field of Industrial Ecology.

As manager of the Digital Laboratory at the Industrial Ecology Programme, NTNU, Trondheim, I am part of a broader effort to increase accessibility to computation and data management for quantitative sustainability research. As researchers, I compile and analyse large data sets to understand the environmental consquences of economic activity.

Besides that I keep a broad interest besides my specific field, in particular to my previous research fields in neuroscience, botany and evolutionary biology.

My full CV (pdf) is available here.

On this page you find an (occasionally) up-to-date list of my publications, some of my presentations and current software and research projects .

Every so often I share some tutorials and thoughts on the blog.
